# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Scrum Masters

### Role Summary
Scrum Masters facilitate agile practices, remove impediments, and coach the team to continuously improve their processes. They ensure the team follows agreed-upon ceremonies and practices while fostering a collaborative environment.

### Responsibilities
- Facilitate daily standups, sprint planning, retrospectives, and reviews
- Remove blockers and shield the team from external interruptions
- Coach the team on agile principles and self-organization
- Track team velocity and help with capacity planning
- Coordinate with Project Managers on cross-team dependencies

### Goals
- Maximize team productivity and focus
- Foster continuous improvement and learning
- Maintain consistent agile ceremonies and practices

### Typical Communication
- Daily facilitation of standup meetings
- Sprint planning and retrospective sessions
- Regular check-ins with team members on blockers
- Coordination with Project Managers on impediments

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Project Managers** to align on timelines and risk management
- Partners with **Developers** to identify and remove blockers
- Collaborates with **Product Managers** on backlog refinement and sprint goals
- Coordinates with **QA Lead** on quality processes and testing workflows

---

## UX Designers

### Role Summary
UX Designers create user-centered designs that balance business goals with user needs. They conduct research, create prototypes, and validate solutions through testing to ensure products are intuitive and accessible.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with developers on implementation feasibility
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design practices
- Validate designs through user feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve satisfaction metrics
- Ensure design consistency across the product

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques with stakeholders
- Collaboration sessions with developers on implementation
- User research findings and recommendations
- Prototype demos and usability test results

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** to understand user needs and business goals
- Collaborates with **Developers** on design implementation and technical constraints
- Works with **QA Lead** to define acceptance criteria for UI/UX features
- Coordinates with **Solutions Architect** on design system architecture
- Engages **Support/Success Managers** for user feedback and pain points

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define and oversee the quality assurance strategy, ensuring products meet acceptance criteria and quality standards. They coordinate testing efforts, manage test environments, and drive quality practices across the team.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and acceptance criteria
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Manage test environments and test data
- Track quality metrics and defect trends
- Mentor team on testing best practices and tools

### Goals
- Ensure high product quality and reliability
- Minimize production defects and regressions
- Improve test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Daily updates in standups on testing progress and blockers
- Test plan reviews with Product and Project Managers
- Defect triage sessions with developers
- Quality metrics reports for stakeholders

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Developers** on test automation and defect resolution
- Works with **Product Managers** to define acceptance criteria and quality standards
- Coordinates with **Project Managers** on testing timelines and release readiness
- Collaborates with **UX Designers** on UI/UX testing and accessibility validation
- Engages **Scrum Masters** to ensure testing is integrated into sprint workflows
- Provides **Support/Success Managers** with quality insights for customer communication

---

## Solutions Architect

### Role Summary
Solutions Architects define technical architecture, integration patterns, and ensure solutions are scalable, secure, and maintainable. They bridge business requirements with technical implementation and provide guidance on architectural decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Define system architecture and integration patterns
- Review technical designs for scalability and security
- Guide technology choices and architectural decisions
- Identify and mitigate technical risks
- Document architectural decisions and patterns

### Goals
- Ensure solutions are scalable, secure, and maintainable
- Minimize technical debt and architectural risks
- Enable efficient integration with existing systems

### Typical Communication
- Architecture review sessions with engineering teams
- Technical design documents and decision records
- Risk assessments and mitigation strategies
- Cross-team technical alignment meetings

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Collaborates with **Developers** on technical design and implementation patterns
- Partners with **Product Managers** to understand requirements and constraints
- Works with **Project Managers** to identify technical dependencies and risks
- Guides **UX Designers** on technical feasibility and performance considerations
- Supports **QA Lead** with test environment architecture and non-functional requirements

---

## Support/Success Manager

### Role Summary
Support/Success Managers serve as the voice of the customer, gathering feedback, coordinating customer communications, and ensuring smooth adoption of new features. They bridge the gap between customers and the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and prioritize customer feedback and issues
- Coordinate customer communications for releases and incidents
- Create customer-facing documentation and training materials
- Track customer success metrics and adoption rates
- Escalate critical customer issues to the delivery team

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Ensure smooth feature adoption and onboarding
- Minimize customer-impacting incidents and issues

### Typical Communication
- Weekly sync with Product and Project Managers on customer feedback
- Customer communications for releases and incidents
- Support ticket reviews and escalations
- Customer success stories and feedback reports

### Interactions with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** to prioritize customer feedback and feature requests
- Collaborates with **Project Managers** on release communications and customer impact
- Works with **Developers** to understand and communicate technical issues
- Coordinates with **QA Lead** on customer-reported defects and reproduction steps
- Provides **UX Designers** with real-world user feedback and pain points
- Engages **Scrum Masters** for process improvements based on support patterns
